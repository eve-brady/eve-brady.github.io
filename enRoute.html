My Projects

enRoute

Working as a group of 5, enRoute was conceptualised built and published within 3 months. EnRoute is an early-stage collaborative travel web-app, for freidns to create itineraries and group trips together from the comfort of their own devices.

During my MSc, I collaborated with a small team to develop a travel app designed to help users plan and manage their trips together. The project's primary objective was to create a fully functioning, database-driven app that combined intuitive design with robust functionality.

I contributed across both front-end and back-end development, collaborating closely on the overall design process. My responsibilities included desiging key web pages, implementing the navigation bar, footer, footer pages and the itinerary editing page. I was also responsible for creating some of the pre-existing itinerary examples featured within the app, which helped to demonstrate the app's functionality and usability to new users.

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience with JavaScript in a front-end context and strengthened my React skills, the primary framework for our backend integration. I also enhanced my proficiency in CSS, HTML, Figma and Canva, while learning to apply design and UX principles to create an intuitive user experience. Using version control and design prototypes allowed me to refine both my technical and teamwork skills. This also allowed me to tackle challenges such as dynamic itinerary management and content integration.
