My Projects

Vyce Games

Vyce games was an early stage start up organisation focused on harnessing the full potential of emerging technologies like Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Artificial Intelligence.

I joined Vyce Games in November 2023 as the Marketing Team Lead and was promoted to Operations Manager by May 2024. During my time with the company, I played a key role in the design and development of our upcoming game mode, CIVS, which was planned for release through a featured partnership with Mineplex in December 2024. Although Vyce Games ultimately shut down, my time there allowed me to gain valuable experience across marketing, operations, and game development.

In addition to my work on CIVS, I also served as the main designer and editor of our website, Vyce Games. I wrote all of our blog posts and maintained the site's design to ensure it reflected our company's aesthetic and branding.
